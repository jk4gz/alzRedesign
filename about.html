Our chapter is comprised of five regions serving 52 counties and cities. Our offices are in Charlottesville, Danville, Lynchburg and Roanoke.

Services

We provide: toll-free HelpLine, support groups, care consultation, lending libraries, Medic Alert + Safe Return, an annual education conference as well as many other programs and services.
