The CWVA Chapter

Region

Our chapter is comprised of five regions serving 52 counties and cities. Our offices are in Charlottesville, Danville, Lynchburg and Roanoke.

Services

We provide: toll-free HelpLine, support groups, care consultation, lending libraries, Medic Alert + Safe Return, an annual education conference as well as many other programs and services.

Our History

In January 2001, the Charlottesville-Piedmont Chapter and the Shenandoah Valley Chapter became the Piedmont-Valley Area Chapter. Later, Shenandoah Valley Chapter (Harrisonburg) and the Central Virginia Lynchburg Chapter and the Blue Ridge Chapter (Roanoke) also became part of the Central and Western Virginia Chapter.

In November 2001, the Central and Western Virginia chapter was created as part of the National Strategic Plan to create one seamless organization that is more effective at providing higher quality programs services that reach more people.
